2024+ Can-Am Spyder F3 & RT Handlebar End Weights
If you own a 2024 or newer Can-Am Spyder F3 or RT and are looking to enhance rider comfort in just a few minutes, you may be interested in our new Weighted Handlebar End Caps. As you may already know, handlebars are naturally prone to amplifying engine and road vibrations due to their long, hollow structure—essentially acting like tuning forks. This can lead to tingling in the fingers, numbness in the hands, and premature fatigue, especially during longer rides. To help address this issue, we've partnered with the team at EvolutionR to bring you a set of heavyweight bar ends that tip the scales at 0.85 lb each, over eight times heavier than the factory units. This added mass alters the resonance characteristics of the bars, effectively absorbing much of the vibration caused by the engine, road surface, and even downshifting. The result is a smoother, more refined ride that enhances overall comfort and feel. Each unit is CNC-machined from corrosion-resistant stainless steel for long-term durability and finished in a glossy black coating for a sleek, factory-style appearance. Installation is a breeze, with a direct bolt-on design that replaces your OEM end caps in just minutes.

Benefits and Features:

  • Designed for 2024+ Can-Am Spyder F3 & RT models
  • Significantly reduces engine, road, and downshift-related handlebar vibration
  • Weighs 0.85 lb each—over 8x heavier than stock bar ends
  • Helps minimize hand fatigue, numbness, and tingling on longer rides
  • CNC-machined from corrosion-resistant stainless steel
  • Gloss black finish for a clean, factory-style look
  • Direct bolt-on replacement for factory end caps
  • Installs in just minutes with basic tools
